Workplace Health and Safety Policy1. Introduction Koursáros is committed to ensuring the health, safety, and well-being of all employees, contractors, visitors, and anyone affected by our operations. We recognize our responsibility to provide a safe and healthy workplace and comply with all relevant health and safety laws and regulations. 2. ObjectivesTo prevent accidents, injuries, and illnesses in the workplace. To provide adequate training and resources to all employees to ensure their safety.To continuously improve our health and safety performance. 3. ResponsibilitiesManagement ensures compliance with health and safety legislation. Provide a safe working environment, including safe systems of work, equipment, and tools.Conduct regular risk assessments and implement necessary control measures. Provide health and safety training to all employees.Investigate all accidents, incidents, and near misses and implement corrective actions.Promote a culture of safety within the organization. Employees must follow all health and safety policies and procedures. Use of personal protective equipment (PPE) as required. Report any hazards, unsafe conditions, or practices to management. Participate in health and safety training and initiatives. Take reasonable care of their own health and safety and that of others. 4. Risk ManagementConduct regular risk assessments to identify potential hazards. Implement control measures to minimize risks. Maintain records of risk assessments, incidents, and corrective actions. Review and update risk assessments regularly and after any significant change in operations. 5. Training and EducationProvide initial and ongoing health and safety training to all employees. Ensure training covers the specific risks associated with their job roles.Keep records of all training sessions and attendees. 6. Emergency ProceduresDevelop and communicate emergency procedures, including fire evacuation plans. Conduct regular emergency drills. Ensure all employees are aware of emergency exits, assembly points, and first aid arrangements. 7. Incident Reporting and InvestigationEstablish a clear procedure for reporting accidents, incidents, and near misses. Investigate all reported incidents to determine causes and prevent recurrence. Maintain records of all incidents and corrective actions taken. 8. Health and Well-beingPromote a healthy work environment and provide support for mental health. Encourage employees to report any work-related health issues. Implement initiatives to promote physical and mental well-being. Encourage employees to seek therapy for general well being, not only for traumatic incidents. 9. Monitoring and ReviewRegularly monitor and review health and safety performance. Conduct workplace inspections and audits. Review the health and safety policy annually or after any significant change in operations. Seek feedback from employees to improve health and safety practices. 10. CommunicationCommunicate health and safety policies, procedures, and updates to all employees. Encourage open communication regarding health and safety concerns. 11. Conclusion  Koursáros is dedicated to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment. We expect all employees to adhere to this policy and contribute to our health and safety objectives..
